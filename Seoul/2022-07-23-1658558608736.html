Gold Spa (황금온천)

SeoulCulture/Art/HistoryRecreational SitesHot Springs & Spa

Overview

Visitors can enjoy hot springs in the middle of the city at Gold Spa in Gangnam, Seoul. The spa not only has high quality water, it also has a jjimjilbang, sauna, movie theater and more. The Gold Spa's waters are known to be particularly effective for skin care and are said to rejuvenate the skin. The unique Golden Pyramid Experience Room provides a special treatment with gold as its wavelength helps heal the body quickly.
