Overview

Daegwallyeong Samyang Farm is a livestock farm opened in 1972 to source product for Samyang Food, then opened as a tourist attraction in 2007 making it open to the public. The animals are free to roam the large ranch fields and the wind generator on top of the hill is the largest in Korea to create natural energy. At the farm, visitors can take in the beautiful scenery, watch a sheep herding performance, feed sheep and ostrich, and so much more. There are various Samyang products sold at the ranch mart as well as organic milk products to try as well.¬†
