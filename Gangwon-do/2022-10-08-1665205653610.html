Pak Kyongni House (박경리문학공원)

Gangwon-doCulture/Art/HistoryRecreational SitesParks

Overview

Pak Kyongni House is a place to explore the life and works of prominent novelist Pak Kyongni. The houses theme was based on Pak Kyongnis well-known novel Toji (The Land).The site used to be her old house before it was converted into a park. Covering an area of 10,578.51 ㎡, the garden and the house have been maintained in their original state. The park also features three themed areas recreated from the book Hongi Dongsan, Pyeongsa-ri Madang, and Yongdurebeol.
