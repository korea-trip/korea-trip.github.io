Overview

The Korean Kimchi Grand Master Kim Soon Ja, Kimchi Theme Park is located in Jeongseon, Gangwon-do. The theme park is dedicated to promoting kimchi to the world. Visitors can learn about the history and development of kimchi as well as other interesting facts. Various programs are also offered for visitors of all ages.
