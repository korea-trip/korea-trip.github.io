Overview

With Seoraksan Mountain to its northeast, Misiryeonggyegok Valley stretches from Yongdae Samgeori to Dojeokpokpo Falls at the valleys peak for approximately 4.8 kilometers. The beauty of Misireonggyegok Valley is as picturesque as those seen from traditional Asian paintings. Misiryeonggyegok Valleys hidden gems are Dojeokpokpo Falls and Chotdaebawi Rock, both rarely known to the public. The valley is also filled with a lush forest that stays cool even in the hot summer months.
