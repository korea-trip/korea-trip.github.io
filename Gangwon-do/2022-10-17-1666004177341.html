Overview

At Wonju Hanji Theme Park, visitors can learn everything about hanji (traditional Korean paper), including its origin and history, through exhibitions, education and activity programs. The museum consists of a two-story main building and an outdoor park. The buildings 1st floor has the Hanji History Room, Hanji Experience Room, an art shop, and a mini library, while the 2nd floor has two exhibition halls and a conference room.
