Yongsopokpo Falls (Yeonhagyegok Valley) (용소폭포 (연하계곡))

Gangwon-doNatureNatural SitesWaterfalls

Overview

Yongsopokpo Falls is a 6-meter high waterfall located in Yeongwol. The water gathers in a small pond, called Yongso Pool. Clear valley streams flow nearby, a refreshing experience for all who venture here. In the summer, the icy cold valley streams flow through verdant, leafy forests and in autumn the entire area is decked in crimson foliage. Yeonhapokpo Falls is located nearby.
