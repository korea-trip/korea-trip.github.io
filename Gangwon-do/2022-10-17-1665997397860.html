Overview

From 1974 to 1995, Wondae-ri Birch Forest has been reforested with approxmiately 690,000 birch trees in an area of 138 hectares, with 25 hectares of land being used as childrens forest experience center. After signing in at the forest entrance, visitors can tour the forest by following the forest walking trail. The forest was designed to promote educational and environmental values and recognize the beauty unique to the birch forest.
