Overview

Vivaldi Parks Ocean World Park is designed to look like an oasis in the middle of the Egyptian desert, in order to give visitors the experience of a cool, refreshing swim under the bright sun. The water park even features a large Sphinx and pyramid. Ocean World is an eco-friendly park popular among all ages. The park is the size of 14 soccer fields, and divided into an indoor zone, extreme zone, wave pool zone, dynamic zone, and megaslide zone.
