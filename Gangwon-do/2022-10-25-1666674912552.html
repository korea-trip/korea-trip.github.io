Overview

Juhyangro, meaning a path of alcoholic flavor, is the name of the tour route around Kook Soon Dangs Hoengseong distillery. The traditional liquor produced by the distillery captures the clean air and water and the sincere devotion of its producers. The distillery offers tours as well as tasting programs, and product sales.
