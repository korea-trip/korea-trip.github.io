The sculpture built to remember the street as the filming location for the Korean TV drama Winter Sonata is located in the middle of Myeongdong Street. In addition, the street has large shopping malls, such as M Department Store and Brown 5th Avenue, cinema, and other attractions. The large-scale underground shopping center established based on the Jungang Rotary, houses some 350 stores where customers can enjoy shopping and relaxation in any weather.
