Overview

Haeundae Cruise in Busan has been in operation since 1979 with courses running from Haeundae's Mipohang Port to Oryukdo Island and back. Along the way, passengers can take in the view of the sparkling sea, the popular Haeundae Beach, APEC House on Dongbaekseom Island, Suyeongman Bay, the oustanding architectual feat of Gwangandaegyo Bridge, and the coastal cliffs of Igidae.

The night cruise course began in 2003, and is a popular event for romantic dates or important family events. This course is perfect for enjoying the nightscape of Haeundae's many skyscrappers, and the lightshow of Gwangandaegyo Bridge.
