Dancing Sea Fountain (춤추는 바다분수)

Jeollanam-doCulture/Art/HistoryExperience ProgramsUnique Experience

Overview

With water shooting up to its peak at 70 meters and a 150-meter water basin, the Dancing Sea Fountain is the biggest sea fountain in the world. The fountain puts on various shows such as music, video, and laser to entertain spectators as they look out to the ocean.
