Overview

Cheongpungho Scenic Monorail covers a 2.6-kilometer stretch of land from Dogok-ri, Jecheon to Bibongsan Mountain (alt. 531 m). Walking to the summit of Bibongsan Mountain from Dogok-ri takes roughly one hour, but it only takes an effortless 20 minutes by monorail. Taking the monorail is a great option to admire the beautiful scenery of Cheongpungho Lake.
