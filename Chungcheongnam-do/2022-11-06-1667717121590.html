Ganggyeong Oknyeobong Park (강경 옥녀봉)

Chungcheongnam-doCulture/Art/HistoryRecreational SitesParks

Overview

Oknyeobong Peak is one of the eight scenic views of Nonsan and offers a great overview of the Geumgang River as it flows through the Ganggyeong district. Continuing out into the distance, views of Nonsan and Iksan can be seen, bringing to mind a traditional painting of a mountain and river. The scenery is so beautiful that there is a myth of mountain gods coming down to play and bathe during the full moon.

The signal-fire tower at the peak was built to receieve signals from the tower on Gwangduwonsan Mountain in Iksan, and passes the message along to Noseong signal-fire tower. The park is also in the area of the nations earliest Baptist church, making it famous for people on a pilgrimage to sacred places.

Directions

[Train]
From Seoul Station or Yongsan Station, take a train to Nonsan Station.

[Express Bus]
From Central City Bus Terminal, take an express bus to Nonsan Express Bus Terminal.

[Bus]
From Nonsan Express Bus Terminal, walk to the bus stop across from Nonsan Intercity Bus Terminal and take Bus No. 707 and get off at Oknyeobong Entrance Bus Stop.

From Nonsan Station, cross the train tracks using the pedistrian overpass, head to the left and take Bus No. 707 from Digital Plaza Bus Stop and get off at Oknyeobong Entrance Bus Stop.
