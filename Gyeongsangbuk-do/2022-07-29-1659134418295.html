Cheongsong Eoreumgol Valley [National Geopark] (청송 얼음골 (청송 국가지질공원))

Gyeongsangbuk-doNatureNatural SitesValleys

Overview

Cheongsong Eoreumgol Valley has a mysterious phenomenon providing a warm breeze in winter and cool breeze in summer. It is one of 20 eorumgol throughout the country. Surrounded by rocks, the valley provides clean water and the man-made waterfall at Tanggeonbong Peak is a popular getaway area during summer period.

Directions

[Bus]
From Dong Seoul Bus Terminal, take an intercity bus to Juwangsan Bus Terminal.
From Juwangsan Bus Terminal, take a Jinbo-Naeryong route bus to Naeryong-ri Bus Stop.
Transfer to Bunam-Okgye route bus and get off at Hang-ri Bus Stop.
Follow the road for 500 m to arrive at Cheongsong Eoreumgol Valley.
