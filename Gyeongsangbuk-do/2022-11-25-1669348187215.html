Overview

Situated in Subi-myeon in Yeongyang-gun, Geommasan National Recreational Forest abounds with phytoncide given off by the trees. This is a place where visitors can engage in programs to discipline the mind and body. The forest offers accommodation facilities, a place for observing indigenous plants, tent sites, outdoor decks, hiking trails, and various other convenience facilities.
