Hakgasan Recreational Forest (학가산자연휴양림)

Gyeongsangbuk-doNatureNatural SitesRecreational Forests

Overview

Hakgasan Recreational Forest is located in a valley on the northern side of Hakgasan Mountain, known for its clean air. The recreational forest has log cabins that harmonize with the nearby trees, creeks, and rocks, making visitors feel as if they are resting at a luxury villa. It is an ideal vacation destination for those who want to get closer to nature. There is a hiking path leading to the summit of Hakgasan Mountain from the forest, taking around 2 hours. Additional amenities within the forest include campfire pits, an outdoor stage, childrens playground, exercise equipment, water play area, a lecture hall, and more.
