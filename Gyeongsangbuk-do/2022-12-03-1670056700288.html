Naeyeonsan County Park (내연산군립공원)

Gyeongsangbuk-doNatureNatural SitesCounty Parks

Overview

Naeyeonsan Mountain in Pohang, Gyeongsangbuk-do is located near the coast. Hywangrobong Peak, the highest peak, is 930 meters above sea level. The mountain has various attractions including ancient Bogyeongsa Temple, 12 waterfalls, and Cheonghagol Valley.
