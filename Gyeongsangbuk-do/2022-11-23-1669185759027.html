Deokgu Spaworld (덕구온천스파월드)

Gyeongsangbuk-doCulture/Art/HistoryRecreational SitesHot Springs & Spa

Overview

Deokgu Spaworld is “Gyeongsangbuk-do Number 1, National Health Hot Spring” accredited by Ministry of the Interior. The spa, located in the middle of Eungbongsan Mountain in Deokgu-ri, has naturally flowing hot spring water of the finest quality in an area of 4,000 pyeong (13,223㎡). The hot spring water features alkalescence at 42.4℃ which is known to be beneficial for people with neuralgia, rheumatism, muscle aches, and skin disorders.

Visitors can see the scene of hot spring waters coming from a natural and clean valley surrounded by uniquely-shaped rocks as well as a beautiful canyon.
