Gyeongju Bomunjeong Pavilion (보문정 (경주))

Gyeongsangbuk-doNatureNatural Sites

Overview

Bomunjeong Pavilion has a beautiful scenery and was listed on CNNs 50 Beautiful Places to Visit in South Korea. The octagonal pavilion and two ponds are surrounded by cherry blossom trees and maple trees making it a must-visit spot all year round.
