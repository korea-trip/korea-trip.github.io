Gyeongju World Amusement (경주월드 어뮤즈먼트)

Gyeongsangbuk-doCulture/Art/HistoryRecreational SitesTheme Parks

Overview

Gyeongju World Amusement operates all year round, with California Beach, a water park in summer, and a large-scale snowpark in winter. The park is complete with thrilling rides, convenience amenities, and restaurants for a full day of fun.
