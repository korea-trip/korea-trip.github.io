Overview

Gyeongju Bird Park offers a unique experience for visitors, just like entering a big birdcage. Guests can touch birds in person and play with them, not just looking at birds inside a cage. The park is designed to give a happy experience for both birds and visitors.

Gyeongju Bird Park, Korean‚Äôs first experiential park featuring flowers and birds alike, is comprised of two floors; the entire first floor is an ecology experience hall that is 90% dedicated to birds and another 10% for other creatures such as reptiles, fish, and small animals. The second floor consists of exhibitions and an experiential hall with outdoor hands-on experience site. The bird park is registered as Gyeongsangbuk-dos top museum and specialized animal park, so it can import and exhibit international rare animal species.
