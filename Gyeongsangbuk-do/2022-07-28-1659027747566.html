Overview

Andong Gunja Village, also known as the Historic Site of Ocheon, is located approximately 20 kilometers north of Andong. The site has around 20 traditional buildings, among which are buildings that were inhabited by the Yean branch of Gwangsan Kim Clan for 20 generations, now designated as a cultural heritage, and traditional houses that were relocated in their original form before the construction of Andong Dam in 1974. Situated mid-section of the mountain, the view of a lake in front is breathtaking. Visitors can see various cultural treasures throughout the village.
