Overview

Located in the southern part of Mireukdo Island, Tongyeong-si, Gyeongsangnam-do, Dara Park wasoriginally named for its resemblance to the tusk of an elephant. The name also means "A vista point for a view of the moon." On the path to the park is Gwanghaejeong Pavilion,which has an broad view of Hallyeohaesang National Park offers a prime locationto watch the sunset.
