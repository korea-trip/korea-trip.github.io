DPIRANG (디피랑)

Gyeongsangnam-doCulture/Art/HistoryRecreational SitesTheme Parks

Overview

DPIRANG is a digital nighttime theme park in Nammangsan Park that consists of 15 walking paths decorated with lights. It became a popular nighttime attraction due to Nammangsan Parks visual transformation while preserving its original scenery.
