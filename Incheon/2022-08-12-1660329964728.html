Overview

Wolmi Theme Park is a popular amusement park complex located on Wolmido Island. It was formerly known as My Land, which first opened in September 1992. It was remodeled and reopened as a 13,223„é°-large theme park in August 2009 under the new name of Wolmi Theme Park. The park has various rides, such as the 70-meter-high Hyper Shoot Drop, Tagada Disco, a two-story Viking Ship, a 115-meter-high Ferris Wheel, and other thrilling attractions.

The theme park has made appearances in several variety shows like "We Got Married," "Two Days and One Night," "Running Man," and more.
