Eobigyegok Valley (어비계곡)

Gyeonggi-doNatureNatural SitesValleys

Overview

Eobigyegok Valley of Eobisan Mountain is a geographically important site, as it sets the boundaries for Gail-ri, Seorak-myeon and Yongmun-ri, Okcheon-myeon, Yangpyeong-gun. ‘Eobi’ (meaning ‘fish flying’) is the name given to this valley, where fish can regularly be seen jumping out of the valley's pristine water. It is also an ideal destination for families with children, as the stream is shallow, the water flows at a gentle pace, the temperature is mild and it is full of pretty pebbles.

Directions

[Bus]
From Dong Seoul Terminal or Sangbong Bus Terminal, take an intercity bus bound for Chyeonpyeong Bus Terminal. From the terminal, take Bus 32-22 or 32-26 bound for Yumyeongsan Mountain Bus Stop.

From Cheongnyangni Transfer Center Bus Stop, take Red Bus 8005 bound for Yumyeonsan Mountain.

Get off at Gail-ri 3Geo-ri Bus Stop and take a taxi to the site (about 10 minutes).
