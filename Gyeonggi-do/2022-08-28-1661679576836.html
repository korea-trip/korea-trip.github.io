Janganmun Gate (장안문)

Gyeonggi-doCulture/Art/HistoryHistorical SitesGates

Overview

Janganmun Gate is the main gate of Hwaseong Fortress at the northern wall. <em>Jangan</em> means capital, implying that Hwaseong Fortress is the secondary capital. Janganmun Gate served as the main gate because the king would pass from this gate when arriving from Hanyang, the capital city, now modern-day Seoul. It is interesting to note that this gate is larger than Sungryemun Gate in Seoul.
